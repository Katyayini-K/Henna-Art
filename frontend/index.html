About the Artist

Hi, I'm Priya a passionate artist, certified dentist, and creative soul behind Priya Henna Styling. With over a decade of experience in henna artistry, makeup styling, and fine art, I've blended my love for detail and design into everything I offer.

From elegant bridal henna to joyful face painting, from traditional Indian styles to playful modern art, I bring a personal touch to every creation. After moving to Switzerland, my passion found new wings reaching clients from different cultures, backgrounds, and ages.

This isn't just a service - it's a way to celebrate individuality, beauty, and art. Whether you want to feel confident on your big day, gift something unique, or just enjoy a creative moment, I'm here to bring your vision to life.
